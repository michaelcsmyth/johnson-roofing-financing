Got Questions?

Financing FAQ

Answers to the most common questions we get about roofing financing.

What is a Same-As-Cash loan?
A Same-As-Cash loan means you have a set period — 90 days, 6 months, or 9 months — to pay off the full balance with zero interest. If you pay it in full before the promotional period ends, it costs you nothing extra. If you don't, the standard 19.99% APR applies to the remaining balance going forward.
How do I apply?
Click the "Apply Now" button on any loan option above. You'll be taken directly to EnerBank's secure online application with Johnson Roofing's contractor profile pre-loaded. You can also call EnerBank at (866) 405-7600, Option 4.
Does applying affect my credit score?
A formal loan application will result in a hard credit inquiry, which can temporarily affect your credit score. EnerBank will inform you of this during the application process.
How quickly will I get a decision?
EnerBank typically provides a credit decision within minutes of completing the online application. In some cases, additional documentation may be requested.
Can I pay off my loan early?
Yes. You can make extra payments or pay off your loan in full at any time without prepayment penalties. Paying off early on a Same-As-Cash loan before the promo period ends means you pay zero interest.
Do I need to choose a loan before getting a quote?
No. You can schedule a free inspection and get your project estimate first. Many homeowners get their quote, decide on a financing plan, and apply all in the same day.
