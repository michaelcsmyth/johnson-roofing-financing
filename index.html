Got Questions?

Financing FAQ

Answers to the most common questions we get about roofing financing.

What is a Same-As-Cash loan?
A Same-As-Cash loan means you have a set period — 90 days, 6 months, or 9 months — to pay off the full balance with zero interest. If you pay it in full before the promotional period ends, it costs you nothing extra beyond the loan principal. If you don't pay it off in time, the standard 19.99% APR applies to the remaining balance going forward.
How do I apply?
Click the "Apply Now" button on any loan option above. You'll be taken directly to EnerBank's secure online application with Johnson Roofing's contractor profile pre-loaded. The application takes about 10 minutes to complete. You can also call EnerBank at (866) 405-7600, Option 4, and reference Contractor ID #75443.
Does applying affect my credit score?
A formal loan application will result in a hard credit inquiry, which can temporarily affect your credit score. EnerBank will inform you of this during the application process. We recommend applying when you're ready to move forward with your project.
How quickly will I get a decision?
EnerBank typically provides a credit decision within minutes of completing the online application. In some cases, additional documentation may be requested, which can add a short review period.
Can I pay off my loan early?
Yes. You can make extra payments or pay off your loan in full at any time without prepayment penalties. Paying off early on a Same-As-Cash loan before the promotional period ends means you pay zero interest.
Do I need to choose a loan before getting a quote?
No. You can schedule a free inspection and get your project estimate first. Many homeowners get their quote, decide on a financing plan, and apply all in the same day. Our team can walk you through the options during your consultation.
